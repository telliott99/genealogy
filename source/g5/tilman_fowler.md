> Tilman Fowler was born in Missouri and in 1848 traveled by covered wagon with his parents and siblings to Gonzales, Texas. He served during the Civil War in Company A, 7th Texas Infantry. He came to California in 1884 and through the years worked as a stock raiser and farmer. He retired in 1921 and lived in Santa Cruz until his death. He was married 1st to Miss Mollie Sharp, 2nd to Mrs Missouri Higgins and lastly to Miss Isabelle Ybarra on March 2, 1896. He was survived by his wife Belle, their son Harold Lee Fowler and seven grandchildren. Tilman was preceeded in death by his first two wives and son Tilman Jr.
