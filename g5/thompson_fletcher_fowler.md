<b>Thompson Fletcher Fowler</b>

b Mar 22 1836 Pike Co., MO

d Jun 14 1886 Bell Co., TX

[Levi Fowler](../g6/levi_fowler.md)

[Dinelah Huff](../g6/dinelah_huff.md)

<hr>

m Martha Smithwick

- Charles Louis Fowler

- Wylie F. Fowler

m [Mildred Maria Kuykendall](mildred_maria_kuykendall.md) Dec 16 1869

<hr>

Buried at Laughlin Cemetery, Milam Co., TX.  

Sibs: see mother [Dinelah Huff](../g6/dinelah_huff.md).

Spent the Civil War in California.  Wylie was born in California.  Charles was sheriff in Santa Cruz, AZ and chief of police in Nogales, AZ, which is probably not a coincidence.
